Why OneReminder?

td;dr

Keep tasks management quick and simple so you can do more, manage less.

OneReminder started as a personal quest to find the fastest way to manage my ever changing tasks.

Working as an inhouse consultant at a Fortune 500 and now CTO of a startup meant my day was often not my own.

Requests come in from various departments at a constant stream. Tasks change, priorities re-evaluated, timelines shifts.

I tried numerious methods from various books, blogs and tools. In the end, I found keeping task management quick and simple made all the difference.

Quick and simple meant less complication and less time wasted managing the tasks. It made me less likely to stop my task management routine. It also means more time to do the actual tasks themselves.

Like those 7 minute workouts, quick and simple works wonders because it's easier to be consistent when the routine is short and painless.

So what is my routine?

  1. Jot things down to keep my mind clear
  2. Plan (not just a todo list, but schedule time)
  3. Do
  4. Adjust and re-evaluate

That's it. I just repeat the above steps throughout the day in the quickest way possible.

This allowed me to learn iOS programming on the side and create OneReminder.
